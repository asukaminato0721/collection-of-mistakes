$$W = \int_{V_{1}}^{V_{2}} {n \frac{RT} V}\,\mathrm{d}V$$

$\begin{cases}
\mathrm{d} Q=\mathrm{d} E+p \mathrm{d} V\\
{Q=\Delta E+∫_{V_{1}}^{V_{2}} p \mathrm{d} V}
\end{cases}$,
$E=n \frac{i}{2} R T$
