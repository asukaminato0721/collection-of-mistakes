$∫_{a}^{b}{\frac{1}{2}DE}\,\mathrm{d}{V}=∫_{a}^{b}{\frac{1}{2}BH}\,\mathrm{d}{V}⟹DE=BH$

$$
∮_{S}\vec{D}⋅\,\mathrm{d}S=Q\\{}\\
D=\frac{λ}{2πr},E=D/ε\\{}\\
U=∫_{a}^{b}{\vec{E}}⋅\,\mathrm{d}{\vec{l}}=\frac{λ}{2πε}\ln\frac{b}{a}\\{}\\

$$

$$
∮_{S}\vec{H}⋅\,\mathrm{d}l=I,H=\frac{U/R}{2πr}
$$
